OUR APPROACH

Maintaining as much of the original style and feel as possible
While bringing the content up to 16:9 HD standards

Artist led approach 

Our team is artist led, which means we have a heavy focus on and are actively attempting to maintain the original artistic vision for the piece               

Industry standard techniques

Rather than using the latest gimic or tecnology preview we have used well established techniques in order to preserve as much of the original detail as possible

Hand painting 

In order to achieve the 16:9 widescreen we have meticulously hand painted additional content matching our work as closely to the original as possible
